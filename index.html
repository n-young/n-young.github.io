About Me

Hey!

My name is Nick Young, and welcome to my website. This page serves a number of purposes: branding, portfolio, a fun project, et cetera (in reality, it's just coding practice). If nothing else, I'll take it as a chance to introduce myself!

I'm currently a high school senior living in Vancouver, Canada, land of the brave. Hoping to study computer science in university, I spend a great deal of time organizing conferences, debating, programming, shooting photo and video, and playing the ukulele. My favourite people live in Langley, New York, and Hong Kong.

While I may not be a Singaporean billionaire, I hope there's something here for you. Please feel free to look around my work and give me a shout if you'd like to connect - I'd like to get to know you as well!
