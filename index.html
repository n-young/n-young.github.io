About Me

Hey!

My name is Nick Young, and welcome to my website. This page serves a number of purposes: a resume, a portfolio, a fun project, et cetera. But if nothing else, I'll take it as a chance to introduce myself!

I'm currently a high school senior living in Vancouver, Canada, land of the north. Next year, I'll be studying computer science at Brown University. I spend a great deal of time organizing conferences, debating, programming, shooting photo and video, and playing the ukulele, among other fruitless pastimes. As a point, my favourite people live in Langley, New York, Providence, and Kingston.

While I may not be a Singaporean billionaire, I hope there's something here for you. Please feel free to look around my work and give me a shout if you'd like to connect - I'd love to get to know you as well!


Cheers,
