ABOUT

Hey! I'm Nick, a high school senior attending St. George's School in Vancouver, BC. A proud "MUN-er", "debater", "programmer", and "hobbyist", I pride myself on my plethora of abilities, rather than my propensity towards any particular skill. In short, nice to meet you! I specialize in nothing.

I've been involved in planning MUN conferences and science fairs, and I'm just starting to dabble in web design and programming. I think the guitar is an amazing instrument, as is the piano, and my free time is usually spent in politically-charged books. Feel free to contact me!

CODE

I'm a beginner programmer, with experience in Java, Python, and the holy trinity of web design: HTML, CSS, and Javascript. While my experience may be scarce, I've applied programming to a variety of projects, particularly web-based ones.

OpenChair, a new Model UN chairing platform, is currently under development, as is Backstab, a program for managing "Spy vs Spy" matches. In the future, I hope to develop fuller web apps and websites.

PHOTO

I shoot on a Nikon D7100 with a kit lens, a 55mm prime lens, and a [wide angle] lens. I mainly do conference photography, but have branched out to urban and city photography as well.

Mostly, I shoot for fun, and not that often. Critique is welcome!
